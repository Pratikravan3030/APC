(2)Vs= |V| set of all vertices 
(3)construct a proprity queue containing all the edges in E in ascending order of the weight
(4)for each V belongs to V add {V} to Vs
(5)while(|Vs|>1){

    choose <v,w> from queue having least weight
    if(<v,w> belongs to 2 different sets in Vs as w1 and w2){
        replace w1 & w2 in Vs as w1 U w2
        add(v,w) in T
    }
    else if(<v,w> are in same set){
        it suggests a cycle
        discard(v,w)
    }
}
